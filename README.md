# FlavorForge AI

FlavorForge AI is an intelligent culinary assistant that provides recipe generation, food analysis, and makes food preparation more efficient by suggesting recipes using Artificial Intelligence.

FlavorForge AI is not like other websites where you just get suggestions for recipes. Instead, it analyzes the user's ingredients and cooking needs and creates customized recipes.

## Features

* **Recipe Generation** – Make recipes based on the ingredients you have in your kitchen.
* **Various Cooking Modes** – Select from the following cooking modes:

  * Use Only My Ingredients
  * Maximum Flavor
  * Healthy Version
  * Budget Mode
  * Restaurant Mode
  * Experimental Chef Mode
* **Food Image Analysis** – Upload an image of the food to determine what dishes it is, its ingredients, type of cuisine, and how it was cooked.
* **Recipe Recreation** – Create exact, healthy, budget, or ingredient matching versions of dishes based on food images.
* **Ingredient Substitution** – Find alternatives for ingredients that you don't have.
* **Flavor Intelligence** – Analyze the flavors and give suggestions for improvement.
* **Cookability Score** – Assess if the recipe can be created considering the available ingredients.
* **Nutrition Analysis** – Look at the amount of calories, protein, carbohydrates, fat, and fiber in it, while having the option to optimize the nutrition.
* **Culinary Chat Assistant** – Ask about cooking related things.

## Working Principle

FlavorForge AI uses an AI reasoning pipeline consisting of the following steps:

1. **Ingredient Intelligence** – Process and understand ingredients.
2. **Recipe Knowledge Engine** – Retrieve knowledge about the culinary world.
3. **Vision Engine** – Extract information from the images of food.
4. **Recipe Generation Engine** – Create personalized recipes.
5. **Flavor Intelligence Engine** – Evaluate and optimize flavors.
6. **Substitution Engine** – Give suggestions for substituting the ingredients.
7. **Cookability Engine** – Measure how feasible is the recipe to cook.
8. **Nutrition Engine** – Calculate nutrition.
9. **Culinary Assistant** – Provide assistance with cooking.

FlavorForge AI intends to become an intelligent cooking companion that will help you to cook better, save food, learn cooking techniques and make wise food choices.
